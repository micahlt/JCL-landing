The AHS Latin Club

The Arlington High School chapter of NJCL is run by Christopher Wheat. It has over 20 members and has won many awards at the Tennessee Latin Convention, including Olympica prizes.








TJCL Convention


The 2020 Tennessee Latin Convention will take place at the University of Memphis between Friday, April 24 and Saturday, April 25. The theme is Omnes...summa ope niti decet, ne vitam silentio transeant, a quote from Sallust that means, "It benefits all to strive with greatest effort, lest they pass their lives in silence". If you're interested in coming, please download the official packet and email our chapter's JCL sponsor.
